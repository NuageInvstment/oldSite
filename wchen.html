Language: English, Chinese (Mandarin)

As a Financial Analyst, Wilbur equips broad knowledge of many types of financial products as well as investment strategy. He specializes in analyzing active and passive real estate investment opportunity and portfolio management. Wilbur Helps clients to evaluate the investment preference and provides the best solution with due diligence.


Prior to Nuage, Wilbur has over four years of work experience in the financial industry. He worked at UG Investment as a Hedge Fund Trader and Research Analyst for over a year and a half. He also helped the Boston College endowment to manage the live stock portfolio for over eight months. With passion to investment analysis, he is working on the CFA certificate and he is currently a level three candidate.

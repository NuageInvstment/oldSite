Vanessa Truong

CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™

Real Estate Investment Advisor

Language: English, Chinese (Mandarin/Cantonese), Vietnamese
E: vtruong@nuageinvrealty.com
P: 1-508-654-0789


Van has always enjoyed all aspects of real-estate. She loves learning about the current market trends, understanding geographic locations, meeting and establishing relationships with clients, and helping them with one of the most important decisions in their life– finding their home.


Van is of Chinese decent and was born and raised in Vietnam. She speaks fluent Vietnamese and Chinese (both Cantonese and Mandarin). Van believes that her cultural background has enriched her life personally as well as professionally.


Van graduated from Clark University with a B.A. in Business Management and M.S. in Finance. Van is also a Certified Financial Planner TM and is a member of the Financial Planning Association of MA. As a result of her career and educational background, she has acquired a deep understanding of all aspects of personal finance and investment that are unmatched by most other real estate professionals. She delicates her time and effort toward helping people make smart financial decisions and plan for their future.


Van purchased her first condo in South Boston three years ago. She loves the neighbor and prides herself on making one of the smart decisions in real estate investment. Van recently purchased her second property in Malden, MA and has live there since. When buying, selling, or renting a Home, you want a professional that you can trust and who understand the big picture of your financial life.

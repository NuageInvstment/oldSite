Emily Ji

Real Estate Investment Advisor

Language: English, Chinese (Mandarin)
E: eji@nuageinvrealty.com
P: 1-517-488-6368


Years of education and multi-culture working experience in finance industry, Emily realized the huge markets in real estate and the large demands for the real estate investment. She is familiar with Greater Boston, Allston, Arlington, Cambridge, Brookline, Brighton, Somerville, Watertown and Waltham. She enjoys spending her time on educating, advising and indicating what the clients want and building up relationships with clients and providing professional advices about real estate and personal finance.


Prior to Nuage, Emily was interned as a financial associate in Financial & Taxation Services dealing with clients’ Tax-return forms. She is a member of Certified Financial Association Institute and approaching her Certificated Financial Analyst.


Emily was born and raised in Northeastern China. She came to United State in 2008 and graduated from Michigan State University with a B.A. in Finance and a Master Degree in Finance from Bentley University.


The words she always keep in mind,” People know you, like you and trust you”, that is what she truly believes and what she really tries to do. When invest in real estate may become the biggest investment in the entire lifetime, whoever not want to find a person who they know, they like and they trust.

Kim Walker

Real Estate Investment Advisor

Language: English
E: kwalker@nuageinvrealty.com
P: 1-617-680-2733


Kim is a Real Estate Investment Advisor who has over 5 years of experience in the real estate industry. Her expertise includes acquiring and advising others on investment opportunities in commercial and residential real estate. Having been born and raised in the north shore area of Massachusetts, Kim has a strong familiarity of many of the local towns and communities.


In 2008, Kim began her career in real estate investing when she acquired her first rental property and became a landlord. Since then, Kim has been honing her skills and her passion for real estate each day. She enjoys exploring all of the opportunities it brings for the investor. She has acquired other investment properties since then and is achieving success.


“I love how each day brings exciting new changes to our industry. There are always new avenues to explore in real estate. It just depends on the timing, the current climate and what is going on at the moment in your own backyard,” she explains.


In addition to her real estate experience, Kim also has a background in the financial investment industry for over the past 10 years. She began advising clients and helping them to reach their financial goals as a Financial Advisor for Ameriprise Financial back in 2003 where she established a large clientele.


Later, she continued her work in the financial industry and became a Learning and Development Business Consultant for Morgan Stanley and LPL Financial.


Kim has a bachelor’s degree from Regis College in Weston, Massachusetts where she majored in English literature. She lives with her husband, Mark and their yellow labrador retriever, Chester. She enjoys travel, skiing, gourmet cooking and is a wine enthusiast.

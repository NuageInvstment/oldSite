CAREERS

We are always looking for talented candidates to join our team. Please contact info@nuageinvrealty.com for more information on the career opportunities below.


Management Position

We are looking for someone with prior real-estate and management experience. Ideal candidate must be self-motivated with excellent interpersonal skills and an entrepreneurial spirit. Fluency in Chinese and English is a must. He/she will be managing a team of advisors on daily operations and transactions.


Advisory Position

Main responsibilities will include, but not limited to, developing and maintaining client relationships, presenting residential/commercial real-estate properties, and advising clients with brokerage transactions. Potential leads and one-on-one training is available. Must currently possess or be planning to pursue a real-estate license in the near future. All compensation is commission based. F1/H1B visa not eligible, OPT eligible.


Internship Position

Comprehensive internship program that provides an exploration of all areas of operations within a small business. Concentration areas include: accounting, finance, taxation, marketing, and communications. Main responsibilities may vary depending on the candidate's strengths and interests. This is an unpaid position with bonus opportunities.

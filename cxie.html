Christie XIE

CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™, REALTOR®, Series 7 and 66 Licensed.
Registered MA real-estate broker and registered representative of Liberty Tree Advisors, LLC, member FINRA, SIPC.
Notary Public.

Founder & CEO

Language: English, Chinese (Mandarin)
E: cxie@nuageinvrealty.com
P: 1-617-678-3889


Christie started her real-estate career in 2010 after her first home purchase in Allston, MA. She founded Nuage Investment Realty, LLC in 2013 to offer consulting and brokerage services to retail and institutional clients. She owns and manages various properties in the greater Boston area in her own portfolio.


Christie was born in Beijing, China. When she was 9, she moved to Quebec, Canada where she enrolled in French-speaking schools. After spending 6 years in Canada, she followed her family to the US and have since lived in Newton, Massachusetts. While being raised in America gave her a sense of independence and an open-mind, her Chinese roots taught her to become a hard-working and self-motivated individual.


Graduated from Babson College in 2007 with a Bachelors in Business Management, she started her career at Fidelity Investments, one of the biggest mutual fund companies in the Financial Services industry. During her 6 years of tenure, she was promoted twice and received numerous nominations for excellence. In addition to passing the Series 7, 63, 66 and two MA insurance licensing exams, she also enrolled at Bentley University where she received her Masters in Taxation. In 2012, she completed full course requirements and examination as a Certified Financial Planner.
